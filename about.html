Behind the Emptiness

Created by artist Lang and released in partnership with Pop Mart, the Hirono series has captured the hearts of collectors worldwide. Unlike traditional designer toys that project joy or excitement, Hirono is a reflection of quiet solitude, nostalgia, and emotional truth.

Lang's design philosophy centers on the idea of preserving the child within all of us—the child who experiences fear, curiosity, loneliness, and wonder without pretense. Each Hirono figure represents a unique state of mind, beautifully hand-painted and sculpted to evoke personal memories and feelings.

At Little Mono, we curate authentic Hirono figurines for passionate collectors. We believe that art toys are not merely decorative items, but emotional companions that anchor our experiences in physical form.
